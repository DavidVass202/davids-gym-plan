Week Foundation
How to progress ❯

Same four sessions every week — progress comes from adding load, not changing exercises. When you complete the top of the rep range on all sets with clean form, make the smallest jump next time: 2.5 kg on the bar, or the next dumbbell up.

The 100 kg bench

Keep the barbell bench first on Session 2 while you're fresh. Build to a solid 3×5, then drive the top set heavier. Add 2.5 kg whenever all fives feel strong — patience beats grinding.

Foundation: leave ~3 reps in the tank, nail technique. Build & Push: ~1–2 in reserve, add weight. Week 7 peaks. Week 8 deloads — drop to 2 sets, lighter weights, skip the heavy sprints/plyos. You'll rebound stronger.

The outdoor work

Plyometrics and sprints go first, on the upper-body days, so your legs are fresh and the reps are explosive. Quality over fatigue — stop a set the moment power drops off.
